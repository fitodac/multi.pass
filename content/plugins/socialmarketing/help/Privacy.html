FarReach.es Plugin and Service

The Social Marketing Plugin ("Plugin") available at http://wordpress.org/plugins/socialmarketing/ collects and transmits to the FarReach.es ("Service") information that you supply and from your WordPress configuration. This information includes:

  1. Your site's Authors, Editors and Administrators.
    • This information is used to attribute content authorship and who published the content.
    • Synchronized security - if you add/remove a user's access to your website - the Plugin communicates that security change to the Service
    • Technical errors that require contacting you or the affected user
    • Marketing additional services to your designated Marketing person
  2. Published Posts: Absense of bugs, the Plugin transmits to the FarReach.es Service, only published, non-password-protected, public posts in categories marked as to be sent to social media sites. Posts that were previously transmitted that then are recategorized, trashed, or rendered not visible to the general public have their id transmitted (but not their content) to the Service so that the Service no longer publicizes that post.
  3. WordPress Categories: The Service routes content based on your categories so category information is necessary for that process to function

The FarReach.es Service ("Service") accesses your social media sites for the sole purpose of publishing content that has been explicitly authorized by your staff. The Service does not access any of your contacts, address book or connections.

Your Website Visitor Privacy: The Plugin does NOT collect any information about your site's web visitors.

FarReach.es website

Like most website operators, FarReach.es collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. FarReach.es's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how FarReach.es's visitors use its website. From time to time, FarReach.es may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

FarReach.es also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. FarReach.es does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to FarReach.es's websites choose to interact with FarReach.es in ways that require FarReach.es to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that FarReach.es gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who use our forums to provide a username and email address. FarReach.es does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

FarReach.es may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. However, FarReach.es does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. FarReach.es uses cookies to help FarReach.es identify and track visitors, their usage of FarReach.es website, and their website access preferences. FarReach.es visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using FarReach.es's websites, with the drawback that certain features of FarReach.es's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
